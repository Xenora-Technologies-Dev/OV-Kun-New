DWC (Double-Wall Corrugated) Pipes

Lightweight High Strength

Innovative double-wall corrugated pipes offering superior strength-to-weight ratio. Perfect for applications requiring flexible, durable, and cost-effective solutions.

Key Features:

  • Exceptional flexibility and bend radius
  • Superior impact resistance
  • Cost-effective alternative
  • Quick installation capability
  • ISO 13238 & EN 13476 certified
Applications:
