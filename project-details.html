HT Safe® Acoustic PP Pipe Installation – Premium Residential Tower

This flagship project showcases the installation of Ostendorf HT Safe® acoustic PP pipes across a 24-storey premium residential tower in Kochi, Kerala. The soundproof drainage system was specified to meet stringent noise reduction requirements for luxury apartments, delivering exceptional acoustic performance throughout the building.

OV Kunststoffe provided comprehensive project support, including BOQ preparation, material scheduling, and on-site installation guidance. The complete HT Safe PP pipe and fittings system was supplied with full DIN/EN compliance documentation and test certificates.

The acoustic performance of the Ostendorf HT Safe system exceeded our expectations. OV Kunststoffe's technical team was instrumental in ensuring the correct specification and seamless installation across all 24 floors.

Anil Sharma

MEP Consultant

The project utilized over 3,000 meters of HT Safe acoustic PP pipes and 1,500 fittings, covering soil, waste and rainwater drainage systems. The chemical resistance properties of the PP material ensured long-term durability against household chemicals and cleaning agents.

This installation demonstrates how Ostendorf's German-engineered soundproof pipes deliver measurable noise reduction in high-rise residential buildings, setting a new benchmark for acoustic comfort in Indian real estate development.
