About OV Kunststoffe

Established 2015

Our Story

OV Kunststoffe Industries Private Limited is India's exclusive distributor of Ostendorf acoustic PP pipes and drainage systems. Founded with a vision to bring world-class German pipe engineering to the Indian market, we have established ourselves as the trusted partner for architects, MEP consultants, builders and contractors seeking premium soundproof piping solutions.

  • Exclusive Indian distributor of Ostendorf PP pipes and fittings
  • Complete range of acoustic, soundproof and low noise drainage systems
  • Pan-India distribution with offices in Kerala and Dubai

Our commitment to quality and technical excellence has made us the preferred choice for premium PP pipe installations in hospitals, luxury residences, commercial towers and institutional buildings across India.

Our Commitment to Excellence in Acoustic Piping

We bring the finest German-engineered PP pipe systems to India, backed by decades of Ostendorf manufacturing expertise. Our comprehensive solutions cover everything from acoustic drainage to chemical-resistant piping for demanding applications.

German Engineering

Exclusively imported Ostendorf PP pipes manufactured to the highest DIN/EN standards with superior acoustic performance.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes rigorous testing and comes with full compliance documentation and test certificates.

Technical Excellence

Our trained engineers provide specification support, installation guidance and acoustic performance verification.

Customer Focus

Dedicated account management, timely delivery and responsive after-sales service for every project.

Comprehensive PP Pipe Solutions for Every Application

From high-rise residential towers to hospital complexes, our Ostendorf acoustic PP pipe systems deliver outstanding noise reduction and long-term durability. We partner with leading developers and contractors across India.

Residential Projects

Soundproof drainage for luxury apartments, villas and residential towers across India.

Commercial Buildings

Acoustic PP pipe systems for offices, hotels, shopping centers and mixed-use developments.

Healthcare Facilities

Low noise and chemical resistant piping solutions for hospitals, clinics and laboratories.

Industrial Applications

Heavy-duty PP pipes for factories, processing plants and industrial drainage requirements.
