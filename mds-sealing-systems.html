MDS Sealing Systems

MDS advanced sealing systems are engineered for maximum performance in the most demanding industrial environments. With precision-engineered elastomer gaskets and proprietary seal designs, MDS delivers superior pressure resistance, chemical compatibility, and long-term durability.

Why Choose MDS Sealing Systems?
  • ✓ Pressure ratings up to 16 bar
  • ✓ Proprietary elastomer compound (FKM, EPDM, NBR)
  • ✓ Chemical resistance across broad spectrum
  • ✓ Temperature range –40°C to +100°C
  • ✓ Precision-engineered manufacturing tolerance ±0.05mm

Technical Specifications

Seal Materials & Grades
Primary Materials: FKM, EPDM, NBR
Seal Types: Pipe gaskets, ring seals, face seals
Size Range: DN 40 – DN 630 (custom available)
Custom Sizes: Yes, full bespoke manufacturing
Compression Set: ≤ 25% (per ASTM D395)
Manufacturing Tolerance: ±0.05 mm (ISO class IT5)
Performance Standards
Pressure Rating: Up to 16 bar (standard)
Temperature Range: –40°C to +100°C (extended available)
Chemical Resistance: Broad spectrum (oils, fuels, coolants)
Leakage Rate: < 10⁻² cm³/s
Standards Compliance: DIN EN 1254, ISO 6926
Warranty: 20-year performance guarantee
