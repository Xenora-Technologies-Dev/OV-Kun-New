DWC Corrugated Pipes

Ostendorf DWC corrugated pipes represent the next generation of flexible drainage solutions. These lightweight, versatile pipes provide exceptional durability and cost-effectiveness for a wide range of applications from infrastructure projects to cable protection systems.

Why Choose DWC Pipes?
  • ✓ 40% lighter than solid-wall alternatives
  • ✓ Superior flexibility for easy installation
  • ✓ Cost-effective without compromising quality
  • ✓ Chemical resistant (pH 2–12)
  • ✓ Long-term UV stability
View Specifications Request Quote

Technical Specifications

Pipe Properties
Material: Polypropylene (PP)
Pipe Type: Corrugated (Double-wall)
Diameter Range: DN 40 – DN 630 mm
Wall Thickness: 1.8 – 3.2 mm
Flexibility: High bend radius (min 25D)
Weight: 40–50% lighter than solid pipes
Performance Standards
Pressure Rating: 0.3 – 2.0 bar
Temperature Range: –10°C to +60°C (–40°C special)
Chemical Resistance: pH 2–12
Standards: DIN EN ISO 6256
UV Stability: Long-term protection
Warranty: 20-year standard

Primary Applications

Road Drainage

Subsurface & perimeter drainage for highways, roads, and parking areas

Cable Protection

Underground electrical and telecommunications cable ducts

Sewer Systems

Gravity sewer and stormwater drainage networks

Agriculture

Land drainage and field water management systems
