Distributor Requirements

Meet these qualifications to become an authorized OV Kunststoffe distributor

Business Profile

  • Registered business entity with valid GST and trade licenses
  • Minimum 2 years experience in building materials/construction supplies
  • Established network of retailers and contractors
  • Ability to maintain minimum inventory levels

Infrastructure

  • Adequate warehouse space for inventory management
  • Transportation capability for product distribution
  • Professional sales and customer service team
  • Office setup with communication infrastructure

Financial Capability

  • Minimum working capital for initial orders
  • Financial stability and credit worthiness
  • Bank references and business credit history
  • Ability to invest in marketing and customer support

Market Presence

  • Committed to market development and sales growth
  • Territory specific sales targets and plans
  • Strong customer relationships and references
  • Willingness to participate in training programs
